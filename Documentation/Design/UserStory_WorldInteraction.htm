World Interaction Story

> look

Dirty Stone Corridor

This stone corridor continues to the north, and opens out to a large cavern to the south. The floor is covered with the debris of loose rocks, small bones, and rotting garbage.

Exits: North, East (closed), South, and West (closed).

In the room is: An ebony sword, and a mangy goblin.

> examine swo

There is more than one "swo" here:

1) An ebony sword, in the room.

2) A silver sword, which you wield.

3) A wooden practice sword, in your inventory.

4) A potion of swooning, in your inventory.

> examine 1   or   examine ebony

This ebony sword is etched with the words of a long-forgotten language.

> east

The iron door is closed.

> open door

There is more than one "door" here:

1) A simple wooden door, to the west.

2) A large iron door, to the east.

> open iron door   or   open east   or   open 2

The large iron door is locked.

> examine iron door   or   examine 2   or   ...

A large iron door is set here in the stone wall.

> unlock 2

You do not have the key for the large iron door.

> examine goblin

This mangy goblin hobbles around, quietly grumbling incoherently to itself.

You think you would be a fair fight for a mangy goblin.

> kill goblin

... the mangy goblin falls over, dead.

> get all from corpse

... You get an iron key from the corpse of the mangy goblin.

> unlock 2

You unlock the large iron door with the iron key.

> open 2

You open the large iron door.

> drop key

You drop the large iron key.

> east

You walk east.

Small Treasure Room

This small stone room is rather featureless, save for some scratches upon one of the walls. The walls are otherwise immaculately smooth.

Exits: West (open).

In the room is: A heavy wooden chest.

> get chest

The heavy wooden chest is too heavy for you to pick up.

> open chest

The heavy wooden chest is locked.

> unlock chest

You do not have the key for the heavy wooden chest.

> close door

You close the large iron door from the inside.

> lock door

You lock the large iron door from the inside.

> examine scratch

The scratches in the wall form crude letters, which read gibberish.

> cast comprehend languages

You successfully cast "comprehend languages" upon yourself.

Your ears tingle as your inner voice adopts greater clarity.

> examine scratch

The scratches in the wall form crude letters, which read "My restless watch bores me; entertain me and my treasure shall be yours."

> dance

You dance around the room.

Someone chuckles.

Someone says "Thank you. As promised, silly one, my treasure is now yours."

Someone unlocks the heavy wooden chest.
